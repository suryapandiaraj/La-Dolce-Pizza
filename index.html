Our Menu

Appetizers

Our appetizer menu offers a delightful taste of Sicily, featuring traditional favorites like crispy arancini (rice balls), flavorful caponata (eggplant salad), and fresh bruschetta topped with ripe tomatoes and basil. Each dish is crafted with locally sourced ingredients, embodying the rich, vibrant flavors of Sicilian cuisine. Perfect for sharing, our appetizers set the stage for a memorable meal.

Main Courses

Our main courses feature a delightful array of traditional Sicilian dishes. Enjoy our arancini, golden rice balls stuffed with meat and cheese, or savor the rich flavors of our caponata, a sweet and sour eggplant dish. Indulge in homemade pasta like pasta alla Norma, topped with eggplant and ricotta, or try our fresh seafood, such as grilled swordfish with lemon and herbs. Each dish is crafted with locally sourced ingredients, ensuring an authentic taste of Sicily.

Desserts

Indulge in our delightful selection of traditional Sicilian desserts. Enjoy the sweet, creamy layers of cannoli filled with ricotta and chocolate chips, the delicate almond flavors of soft amaretti cookies, and the refreshing zest of our homemade lemon granita. Each dessert is crafted with the finest local ingredients, offering a perfect end to your authentic Sicilian meal.
